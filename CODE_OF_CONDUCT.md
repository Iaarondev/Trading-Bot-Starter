# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
Create a welcoming environment for all contributors.

## Standards
- Be respectful
- Accept constructive criticism
- Focus on community benefit
- Show empathy

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment
- Trolling
- Personal attacks
- Public/private harassment

## Responsibilities
Maintainers enforce standards and take corrective action.

## Enforcement
1. Warning
2. Temporary ban
3. Permanent ban
